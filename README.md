Hot Desking workplace arrangement is on a rise post COVID-19 where staff follow a hybrid work culture to balance the reduce spread of infection. The Indoor Environmental Quality (IEQ) for an existing office is studied to understand employee work comfort environment in a hot desk arrangement. Work productivity influenced by IEQ factors is investigated to mitigate the work environment problems faced by occupants. An employee satisfaction survey was used as a guide for required indoor environmental quality metrics. Environmental Quality Instrument Cart including a camera, air temperature and humidity sensors, Air temperature, CO2, CO, TVOC and particulate sensors was used to collect data. The Data set was analyzed using various methodologies including statistical analysis and data mining. The result helps to improve the workstation layout for occupants and increase the work productivity.
